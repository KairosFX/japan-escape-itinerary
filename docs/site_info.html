About

Who We Are

Japan Escape is a custom travel-planning site for one Japan route. It combines trip flow, food guidance, weather-aware decision-making, and share-ready pages in a cleaner visual system.

Purpose Help travelers move from inspiration to a usable route without opening ten different tabs and losing the thread. Focused planning

Policy

Privacy Policy

This site is a static travel guide. It does not provide account creation or store private trip data on a server.

What the site stores Small local browser preferences such as language choice and cached weather reads may be stored in your own browser.
What the site does not do No user accounts, no password storage, and no server-side personal profile database are used in this version of the site.
Third-party links External links such as JNTO, Skyscanner, Google Maps, SmartEX, and Hakone transport resources use their own privacy practices after you leave this site.
Policy

Cookie Policy

This site does not currently run an ad-tech cookie system. Browser-side preference storage is used only to keep the experience more consistent.

  • Language selection may be remembered locally in your browser.
  • Weather fallback data may be cached locally so the weather cards still show a saved read if live fetch fails.
  • If ad tools or analytics are added later, this page should be updated before launch.
Policy

Terms Of Use

Use the site as a planning reference, not as an official transport, safety, or weather authority.

Planning guidance only Schedules, prices, and conditions can change. Re-check official providers before travel.
External services Flights, transport bookings, map routing, and official travel info are handled by external providers when you leave this site.
Content use This site organizes travel information and public image references into an original planning experience for the trip.
