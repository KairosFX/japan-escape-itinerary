If Time Expands

More Tokyo, Still A Separate Okinawa Trip

The optional section is now expandable too. Open either card for more detail on how the extra days fit without breaking the tone of the main route.

Day 8 Tokyo Skytree and Solamachi pair naturally because they are in the same complex. Akihabara works well later in the day when signage and store fronts feel more alive.
Day 9 The Imperial Palace area gives you a calmer morning. Shinjuku then shifts the city mood back to density, towers, and station-scale energy.
Extra Facts Tokyo Skytree is the tallest structure in Japan, and Solamachi is the large shopping complex at its base. Akihabara and Shinjuku add very different versions of Tokyo after a Shibuya-first main trip.
Why Separate It The current itinerary is built around rail flow and connected mainland transfers. Okinawa adds flights, beach pacing, and a completely different trip tempo.
What It Protects Keeping Okinawa separate preserves the strong contrast already built into this route: Osaka energy, Kyoto history, Hakone reset, Fuji scenery, and Tokyo finish.
Best Use Save Okinawa for a future trip that can center on coastlines, island transport, diving or beach time, and a slower overall rhythm.
