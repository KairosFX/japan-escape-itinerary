City Food

Food Paired With The Right City

The city cards are organized for fast use. If someone asks what to eat in Osaka or where to keep it practical in Hakone, the answer is visible immediately.

Osaka

Street-Food Start

Best first-night food city for takoyaki, okonomiyaki, kushikatsu, gyoza, and loose late wandering.

  • Areas: Dotonbori for the bright crawl, Shinsekai for kushikatsu and retro-night energy.
  • Sit-down idea: Namba or Ura-Namba for okonomiyaki, izakaya, or yakiniku.
  • Cash feel: Around 3,500 to 6,000 yen usually keeps a food-heavy Osaka day easy.
Kyoto

Slower Specialty Day

Better for matcha, sweets, tofu, soba, and one deliberate sit-down meal than for chaotic late-night snacking.

  • Areas: Nishiki Market for tasting and browsing, Gion or Higashiyama for a calmer meal.
  • Sit-down idea: Soba lunch or tofu-forward dinner in the old-street side of the city.
  • Cash feel: Around 3,000 to 6,500 yen depending on how refined the meal gets.
Hakone

Eat Early, Keep It Easy

Hakone is the place to lean into ryokan food, station snacks, and earlier dinner timing rather than big late-night freedom.

  • Areas: Hakone-Yumoto is the safest practical snack and buy-what-you-need zone.
  • Sit-down idea: Use the included ryokan meal or an earlier local dinner before options narrow.
  • Cash feel: Around 2,500 to 5,000 yen outside lodging-included meals.
Mt. Fuji Area

Practical Scenic-Day Food

Keep Day 6 meals reliable and compact so the clear-weather window stays protected.

  • Areas: Shimoyoshida around the Chureito side or Kawaguchiko for easier fallback cafes.
  • Must-try: Yoshida udon is the cleanest local callout if you want one specific Fuji-area dish.
  • Cash feel: Around 2,500 to 5,500 yen usually covers a practical scenic day fine.
Tokyo

Strong Final Dinner City

Shibuya alone is enough for sushi, tonkatsu, ramen, gyoza, dessert, and late-night fallback food without complicating the last day.

  • Areas: Stay in Shibuya for the main route. Add other districts only if time expands.
  • Sit-down idea: A Shibuya dinner before or after Shibuya Sky keeps the final day clean.
  • Cash feel: Around 4,000 to 8,000 yen leaves room for a better final dinner.
Fast Food Reminder Use this if you only need the short version.
  • Osaka: late and loose food city.
  • Kyoto: slow specialties, not late chaos.
  • Hakone: eat earlier.
  • Fuji: practical lunch, do not lose visibility.
  • Tokyo: strong final dinner and dessert backup.
