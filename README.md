# 🇯🇵 Japan Escape
## City Nights, Fuji Views, Tokyo Finish

A clean 7-day Japan route built around **Osaka energy**, **Kyoto contrast**, **Hakone scenery**, **Mt. Fuji views**, and a **Tokyo Shibuya finish**.

---

## ✨ Overview

This trip is designed to stay **compact, smooth, and memorable**.

Instead of rushing too many cities, the route keeps things focused:
- **Osaka** for atmosphere, food, and nightlife
- **Kyoto** for a full-day cultural contrast
- **Hakone + Mt. Fuji area** for the scenic stretch
- **Tokyo** for the final city landing

---

## 🗺️ Route

**Osaka → Kyoto → Osaka → Shin-Osaka → Odawara → Hakone → Mt. Fuji Area → Tokyo**

---

## 🎯 Trip Style

- Keep **Osaka relaxed**
- Use **Kyoto as a full-day contrast**
- Save the main long transfer for the middle of the trip
- Keep the **Fuji day flexible** in case weather changes
- Finish strong with a simple **Tokyo / Shibuya ending**

---

### Osaka
The starting base of the trip.

**Focus:**
- Nightlife
- Street food
- Tsutenkaku

- Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan

---

### Kyoto
A full-day trip from Osaka.

**Focus:**

---

### Hakone + Fuji
The scenic middle section of the route.

**Focus:**
- Hakone sightseeing

---

### Tokyo
A clean and simple final stop.

**Focus:**
- Shibuya Crossing
- Shopping
- Shibuya Sky at night

---

## 🗓️ 7-Day Itinerary

### Day 1 — Osaka Arrival Mood
- Arrive in Osaka
- Ease into the city
- Explore Osaka nightlife
- Visit **Tsutenkaku**
- Walk around **Shinsekai**

**Vibe:** neon, casual, exciting

---

### Day 2 — Kyoto Day Trip
- Full day in Kyoto
- Focus on the cultural and historic side of the trip
- Enjoy the contrast from Osaka’s city energy

**Vibe:** calm, classic, scenic

---

### Day 3 — Osaka Reset
- Visit **Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan**
- Keep the rest of the day flexible
- Food, walking, and free time in Osaka

**Vibe:** light, fun, no pressure

---

### Day 4 — Move to Hakone
- Travel from **Shin-Osaka to Odawara** by bullet train
- Head into Hakone
- Do light sightseeing
- Stay overnight in Hakone

**Vibe:** transit day, soft reset

---

### Day 5 — Hakone Morning
- Easy morning in Hakone
- Enjoy the slower pace
- Transfer toward the **Mt. Fuji area**

**Vibe:** quiet, scenic, refreshing

---

### Day 6 — Fuji Scenic Day
- Explore **Lake Kawaguchiko**
- Visit **Arakurayama Sengen Park / Chureito Pagoda**
- Stop by **Oshino Hakkai**
- Keep timing flexible depending on conditions

**Vibe:** postcard day

---

### Day 7 — Tokyo Finish
- Travel into Tokyo
- Explore **Shibuya Crossing**
- Shopping around Shibuya
- End the trip with **Shibuya Sky at night**

**Vibe:** big finale

---

## 🚄 Transit Flow

The route keeps the longer transfer concentrated in the middle so the beginning and end stay lighter and more enjoyable.

- Start relaxed in **Osaka**
- Use **Kyoto** as a day trip
- Make **Day 4** the main transit anchor
- Let the **Fuji section stay flexible**
- Finish with a simple **Tokyo landing**

---

## 🌤️ Flex Priority

The **Mt. Fuji area** works best with clear weather, so that section of the trip should stay flexible when possible.

That means:
- avoid overpacking earlier days
- leave room to adapt
- treat the scenic section as the most weather-sensitive part of the route
